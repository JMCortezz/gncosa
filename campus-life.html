Come and discover all the exciting opportunities, fun activities, and helpful services that turn our campus into a welcoming place for students. Today at Guagua National Colleges

At Guagua National Colleges, life on campus is always buzzing with excitement and a strong sense of community. From our lively hangout spots to the thrilling sports events, there's never a dull moment here. We believe in bringing students together, regardless of their backgrounds, to learn, grow, and create lasting memories.

SCHOOL EVENTS

Our school recently hosted a series of exciting events, including GNC Concert Night with 'The Juans,' a Student's Night, a lively GNC Free Sports Clinic, Fellowship Breakfast filled with camaraderie, a proud Color Fun Run, and a heartwarming Foundation Day These events united our school community, and we look forward to more thrilling moments ahead!
