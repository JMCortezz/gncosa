MISSION

To enrich the lives of students through quality educational services and purposeful co-curricular and extra-curricular activities pertinent to any modality of learning.

VISION

The Office of the Student Affairs is an outstanding service-oriented office which provides the needs of the students beyond the classroom setting.

GENERAL OBJECTIVES

The Office of the Student Affairs (OSA) seeks to provide for the overall development of students through programs and services addressing the spiritual (FIDES), intellectual (SCIENTIA), racial (PATRIA), social, physical and personal stewardship areas of life.

Promoting Active Learning

Engage students in active learning, both in-person and online as needed.

Unlocking Student Potential

Help students explore more on their capabilities and resourcefulness.

Fostering Values and Ethics in Students

Help students develop inherent values and ethical standards.

Elevating Student Learning Expectations

Set high and attainable expectations for student learning.

Inquiry-Driven Excellence
