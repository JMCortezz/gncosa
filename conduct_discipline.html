CONDUCT AND DISPLINE

A GNC student practices good manners and right conduct. This is the major thrust of the school’s values education program.

All students shall wear and display their identification cards before entering and while inside the campus.

Uniform is worn in all school functions. Ladies wear blue skirts, white blouses with sleeves of non-transparent materials and closed black shoes. Men wear plain white polo shirt with collar, black pants and black leather shoes. Students who are not in proper attire are not allowed to enter the campus.

In the course of the Blended mode of learning, students are to be notified of the following:

The school requires regular and punctual attendance in all classes. No student is entitled to credit in any subject in which s/he is enrolled unless s/he attends his/her classes regularly. Absence for any cause whatsoever does not exempt student from quizzes, examinations, written work and/or other matters required during the period of his/her absence. The responsibility of making up for such work rests wholly upon the students. Extended absence due to sickness must be certified by a Physician. A student who incurs absences of more than twenty percent (20%) of the prescribed number of class hours or laboratory periods during the semester should be given a failing grade and given no credit for the subject. The school head may at his/her discretion and in exceptional cases exempt a student who exceeds the twenty per-cent limit for reasons considered valid and acceptable to the school. Such discretion shall not excuse the student concerned from responsibility. This discretion is vested in the school head, and may not be availed of by a student nor granted by a faculty member without the consent of the school head.

A student who falls absent is to present to the teacher an excuse letter noted and approved by the Academic Dean. A matrix of students’ absences (Table-1) is to be noted and followed in monitoring the student’s attendance.
