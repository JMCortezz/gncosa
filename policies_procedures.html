PROCEDURES

Students desiring to establish, join and participate in student organizations on campus shall do as a right, subject only to reasonable regulations promulgated by the College through the Dean/Director of Student Affairs. New organizations applying for accreditation must submit to the Office of the Student Affairs (OSA) the following documents for evaluation:

01

Officers list signed by Club Adviser and Dean.

02

Duly ratified Constitution and By-laws.

03

Academic year proposed activities list.

Old organizations applying for re-accreditation must submit all the above requirement, plus the following:

List of two documented activities accomplished in the preceding year, one of which must be of community service.

Financial statements for the first and second semester signed by the Club Adviser and the College Dean.

STUDENT PUBLICATION

The College Mirror is the official publication of the College Department. As the name suggests, the publication reflects the ideas of the GNC academic community. Aside from being a venue for legitimate students views and opinions, the College Mirror also provides students with potential creative and journalistic talents an initial exposure and opportunities for actual application.
