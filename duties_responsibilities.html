DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES OF STUDENTS

Practice moral responsibilities toward self and others within the Christian community.

Promote, preserve, and actively contribute to the general welfare and growth of students' well-being.

Perform duties as provided for in the student manual and the laws of the land.

Abide by the rules and regulations of the College, fostering a respectful, responsible, and inclusive community.ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ

Participate actively in school's extracurricular, civic, religious, and community service activities, online or in-person.

Uphold academic excellence, foster community engagement, and cultivate strong Christian values within our students.

During in-person classes, students must help maintain cleanliness and order in classrooms, libraries, labs, and campus facilities.

Vote wisely in student elections; and actively engage in campus community-building activities as well.ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ

Always diligently protect and safeguard GNC's valuable properties and assets from harm and potential threats.ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ

CAMPUS NORMS

A student of the Guagua National Colleges
should observe the following during face-to-face learning inside the campus:

  • 1. Act and behave always in or out of the school, in a manner befitting a Christian student, always mindful of the good name of the College and fellow student;
  • 2. Develop the values/honor system which stresses personal integrity, dignity, respect, regard for authority, trustworthiness and sense of duty;
  • 3. Help promote and maintain peace and order, cleanliness and orderliness, discipline, respect for one another and harmony within the College premises;
  • 4. Begin and end all classes with prayers led by either the mentor or the student;
  • 5. Return all lost and found items to the Office of the Student Affairs for proper handling;
  • 6. Avoid loitering along corridors while classes are going on;
  • 7. Refrain from sitting on railings and staircases;
  • 8. Observe proper garbage or waste disposal;
  • 9. Observe proper hygiene and sanitation;
  • 10. Avoid tampering notices posted on bulletin boards. Notices are posted and removed with the official permission from the Dean / Director of Student Affairs or the Prefect of Discipline;
  • 11. Avoid eating in places such as the chapel, classrooms, library, laboratory rooms, audio-visual rooms and offices;
  • 12. Avoid public display of affection on campus.
  • 13. Personal letters/correspondence must not be addressed to the school. It is not the duty of the school to deliver the same to students;
  • 14. Refrain from staying in the following places: in any unlighted room or corner on campus and other secluded areas in the school premises; and
  • 15. Cell phones, transceiver and other communication devices should be used in moderation at the right time and place to minimize cheating and annoyance. Phone calls and texting are strictly prohibited in the class, offices, or during any school-related activities.
